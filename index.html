Twas the Night before Christmas
Toastmasters Version

Twas the minute before our favourite Xmas meeting
And all through the zoom room, not a member was bleating
The speeches were ready and prepared with care
In hopes that the Evaluator soon would be there

The members were sitting all comfy on chairs
While visions of contest rules raised their neck hairs
And the chair in her role, and I ready to speak
Had just settled down, with no time left to tweak

When out on the internet there arose such a clatter
I worried and stressed about what was the matter
Away to the zoom master I flew like a flash
Hoping and begging that the system not crash

The sound on the zoom room came in loud and clear
And the members of the club all began to cheer
When, what to my searching eyes should appear,
But many miniatures squares of faces so dear

With an expert person leading as chair
And a Sergeant at Arms starting us on time by a hair
The meeting began to flow without a hitch
The general evaluator taking notes, as neat as a stitch

The rant was on point, and man did they care!
The humorist creative and performed with flair
The timer multi-tasked between computer and phone
It is really is a master skill, taking time to hone

The speakers gave us gifts both grand and great
The evaluators were kind, wise, and compelled to state
That Speak your Mind Toastmasters, the best in the land
Has once again proven itself to be truly grand

Our year has ended, the new year to begin
So we took a moment to wish to our dear kin
Before clicking end meeting, soon to be out of sight
Happy Christmas to All and to all a Good Night!

- Mariah-Jane Thies
TEDx Speaker and SYMian member
