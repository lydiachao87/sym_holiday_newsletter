I have been forgoing wheat and sugar and have this great brownie recipe that is attached.

Favourite Keto Brownies
Wheat and Sugar Free!!
Ingredients

* 1 cup fine almond flour
* 1 / 4 cup cocoa powder
* 2 tbsp dutch cocoa or additional regular
* 1 tsp baking powder
* 1 / 2 tsp salt
* 1 / 3 cup melted coconut oil or butter
* 3 tbsp water or additional oil
* 2 eggs
* 2 / 3 cup granulated erythritol or regular sugar(I use Amason’s…best price on Amazon)
* 1 tsp pure vanilla extract
***For Chocolate-Mint Brownies: Add 1 tsp Peppermint extract

Use a full size lasagne pan and double the recipe, and just cook for a bit longer to get the centre firmer.
Bonuses: Make an avocado based frosting or…super easy…sprinkle chocolate chips on top (to stay with the sugar free use Lily’s chocolate chips)
I use organic coconut oil and grass fed butter…making this now a “health” treat because all ingredients are good for you!!
Preheat the oven to 350 F. Grease an 8-inch pan, or line with parchment. Mix all ingredients well. Spread evenly into the pan ( grease with coconut oil). Smooth down, using a second sheet of parchment if needed (if you care…I don’t)
Bake 20 minutes on the center rack, then let cool completely and they will continue to firm up. I often find I need to do 2-3 minutes longer to see the centre firm especially if I double the recipe.
They also firm up even more if you refrigerate very loosely covered overnight. The brownies are delicious with or without frosting.

I am going to give a recipe for breakfast, it is called "Menemen" in Turkish.
You will need:

*1 tablespoon of olive oil or equivalent amount of butter
* 3 eggs
* 2-3 regular size tomatoes
* 1 pepper
* 1 small or 1/4 big onion
* 1-2 green onions
* salt, pepper, paprika

Chop one mid-size pepper, green is the best but any color is fine. Chop a little onion. If you have a big one, chop the quarter of it. Also chop 1 or 2 green onions. Grate 2-3 regular size tomatoes or dice them.
Put the butter or olive oil in the pan. Fry the pepper and the onion, leave the green onions to the end.
When they seem to have died (means cooked fine), add the grated or diced tomatoes. Cook more. When the water of the tomatoes seems to be gone, add the salt, pepper, paprika, and mix well. Finally add the green onions and eggs. When the eggs are cooked you have the best breakfast. Serve it with a nice cup of earl grey black tea :)
- Pinar
